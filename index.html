About Me

Engr. Edward John Felicia

I am a mechanical engineer currently transitioning to a software engineer. I am currently under Zuitt Bootcamp and about to finish the front-end development with this portfolio as my end project. I am so excited to study the backend side of web development because I feel this is where I can use my problem-solving skills.
Before, I was an engineering management trainee of one of the top food manufacturing companies here in the Philippines. Last year, during the pandemic, I was promoted to production supervisor and was able to win the line of the year award. But with all of that, I am still not satisfied with where I am going and that's why I decided to take a leap of faith, a reset, and study software engineering.
I am very interested in programming, business, finance, and leadership. Recently, I was able to use all my interest as I currently manage a social media page as the marketing coordinator of a molecular laboratory in Metro Manila as we offer RT-PCR Swab test and the like to people who were required by private or public entities for a specific purpose.
As the world was forced to digitalization, I am already adapting and learning to use my skills and interests to tackle real world or business problems through software engineering.
